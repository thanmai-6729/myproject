Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are the admission requirements?

Candidates must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics with minimum 60% marks and qualify in entrance exams like JEE Main or state-level exams.

Do you provide hostel facilities?

Yes, we have separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with modern amenities, mess facilities, and 24/7 security.

What is the fee structure?

Fee structure varies by program. Please contact our admissions office or visit during office hours for detailed information about tuition and other fees.

Are scholarships available?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships and financial aid to deserving students. Government scholarships are also available for eligible candidates.

What is the placement record?

Our college maintains an excellent placement record with 95%+ students getting placed in top companies. We have a dedicated placement cell for training and recruitment.
